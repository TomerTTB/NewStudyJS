How to Play "Pig" - Quick Guide


Objective: The goal is to be the first player to reach a set score, typically 100 points.

Setup: Two players take turns rolling a standard six-sided die.

Gameplay:

  • Roll the die on your turn to accumulate points.
  • Add the points from each roll to your turn total.
  • Decide whether to keep rolling or hold to save your points.

Scoring:

  • Rolling a 1 ends your turn, and you lose the turn total.
  • You can choose to hold at any time, adding your turn total to your overall score.

Winning: The first player to reach or exceed the target score wins.

Strategy Tips:

  • Be cautious when rolling; a 1 resets your turn total.
  • Strategic holding can secure your points and prevent losses.
  • Adapt your approach based on your current score and the opponent's progress.


Have Fun! Enjoy the simple yet engaging dynamics of the Pig game with friends or family.
